A multimodal acquisition setup that fuses **high-density EEG (g.tec)** with **optical motion capture** so that cortical activity and the kinematic events it drives can be aligned at sample-level precision.

**Status**

- Engineered the hardware synchronization solution between the two systems.
- Optimizing data-acquisition protocols to reduce latency between cortical activity and recorded kinematic events.
- Analyzing neural correlates of grasping and reaching movements with an eye toward Brain-Computer Interface (BCI) applications.
